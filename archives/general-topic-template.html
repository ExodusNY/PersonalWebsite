Partial Order Reduction (POR)

Reading time ~6 min · Last updated Jan 2025

Partial Order Reduction (POR) reduces the state explosion in model checking of concurrent system modelling by exploring only a representative subset of interleavings. Classical approaches include persistent sets and sleep sets, with many variants of the POR algorithm used in practice (e.g., tools from LFCS and elsewhere).
